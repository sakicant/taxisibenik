# /stakeholder-update — Stakeholder Update

Draft a concise status update for stakeholders.

## Workflow

1. **Gather context** — Read recent git activity, task updates, context files
2. **Identify audience** — Who is this for? (team, leadership, board, client)
3. **Draft update** — Using the format appropriate for the audience

## Format

### For leadership/board (under 150 words)
**TL;DR:** [One sentence headline]
**Status:** 🟢 On track / 🟡 At risk / 🔴 Blocked

**Progress:**
- [Completed item with impact]

**Next:**
- [Planned item with timeline]

**Risks:**
- [Risk with mitigation]

**Ask:**
- [Specific help needed]

### For team (under 100 words)
**Done:** [What shipped]
**Doing:** [Current focus]
**Blocked:** [What needs unblocking]

## Verification
- Is the most important news first?
- Would the reader know what action to take?
- Are numbers and dates accurate?

Usage: `/stakeholder-update [audience] [project or scope]`
