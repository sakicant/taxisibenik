# /campaign — Plan Campaign

Plan a marketing or outreach campaign end-to-end.

1. Clarify the campaign goal, audience, and timeline
2. Check context files for brand voice, current strategy, and metrics
3. Develop the campaign plan:
   - **Goal and success metrics**
   - **Target audience** with segmentation
   - **Core messaging** and key proof points
   - **Channel plan** (which channels, what content, when)
   - **Asset list** (everything that needs to be created)
   - **Timeline** with milestones
   - **Budget estimate** if applicable
4. Present as a ready-to-execute brief

Usage: `/campaign [description]`
Examples:
- `/campaign product launch for Language Grounding`
- `/campaign ABM sequence for top 10 enterprise accounts`
