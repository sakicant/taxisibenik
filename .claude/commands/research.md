# /research — Topic Research

Research a topic and produce an actionable summary.

1. Search for recent, credible information on the topic
2. Cross-reference multiple sources
3. Produce a structured summary:
   - **Key findings** — The important facts
   - **Sources** — Links to everything referenced
   - **Analysis** — What this means for us
   - **Recommendations** — Suggested next steps
4. Save the summary to outputs/ if the user requests

Usage: `/research [topic or question]`
Examples:
- `/research competitor pricing for enterprise tiers`
- `/research best practices for employee onboarding in 2026`
