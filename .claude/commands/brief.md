# /brief — Create a Brief

Generate a concise brief from available context and information.

1. Read relevant context files and any specified source material
2. Identify the audience and purpose of the brief
3. Structure the brief:
   - **Executive summary** (1 paragraph)
   - **Background** (what the reader needs to know)
   - **Key points** (3-5 bullets)
   - **Recommendations or next steps**
   - **Open questions** (if any)
4. Keep it to one page maximum

Usage: `/brief [topic or purpose]`
Examples:
- `/brief board update on Q1 marketing performance`
- `/brief competitor landscape for the sales team`
