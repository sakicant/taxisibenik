# Visual Ad Formats

Visual formats are the production structure of an ad or content piece. What it looks like, how it is built, and what medium it lives in. Formats are the vessel. Creative mechanics (ad-concept-developer) define the cognitive move inside.

## Where Formats Sit

```
Messaging Angle (creative-strategy-mapper)
    ↓
Creative Mechanic (ad-concept-developer)
    ↓
Hook (scroll-stop-hooks)
    ↓
Visual Format (this skill)
```

Format and mechanic are bidirectional. Start with a format and work backward to find the mechanic, or start with a mechanic and find the format that delivers it.

## Video Formats

### Talking Head
One person speaks directly to camera. Authentic, personal, scalable.
- **Medium:** Video
- **Funnel:** All stages
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Social Witness, Reframe, Trojan Horse
- **When to use:** When credibility or personal connection matters. Fastest format to produce.

### UGC (User-Generated Content)
Content that looks like it was made by a real user, not a brand. Shot on phone, natural lighting, casual delivery.
- **Medium:** Video
- **Funnel:** Unaware, Problem-Aware
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Overheard Conversation, Trojan Horse, Social Witness
- **When to use:** When ad fatigue is high and the audience scrolls past anything that looks produced.

### Product Demo
Shows the product in action. What it does, how it works, what the experience is.
- **Medium:** Video
- **Funnel:** Solution-Aware, Product-Aware
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Contrast Without Comment, Implied Answer
- **When to use:** When the product's value is best understood by seeing it work.

### Screen Recording / Walkthrough
Captures the actual product interface. Cursor movements, clicks, real workflow.
- **Medium:** Video
- **Funnel:** Solution-Aware, Product-Aware
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Trojan Horse (educational framing), Contrast Without Comment
- **When to use:** Software products, SaaS, tools. When "show, do not tell" is the strategy.

### Before / After
Shows the transformation from problem state to solution state.
- **Medium:** Video or Static
- **Funnel:** Problem-Aware, Solution-Aware
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Contrast Without Comment, Social Witness
- **When to use:** When the visual difference between states is dramatic and self-evident.

### Reaction / Response
Someone reacts to the product, a result, or a claim in real time.
- **Medium:** Video
- **Funnel:** Unaware, Problem-Aware
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Social Witness, Implied Answer
- **When to use:** When genuine surprise or delight is more convincing than explanation.

### Interview / Testimonial
A customer or expert answers questions on camera.
- **Medium:** Video
- **Funnel:** Product-Aware, Most-Aware
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Social Witness
- **When to use:** When social proof needs depth beyond a quote card.

### Skit / Scenario
A scripted scenario that dramatizes the problem or solution.
- **Medium:** Video
- **Funnel:** Unaware, Problem-Aware
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Borrowed Enemy, Overheard Conversation, Reframe
- **When to use:** When humor or exaggeration makes the pain point more relatable.

### Voiceover + B-Roll
A narrated story or explanation over lifestyle or product footage.
- **Medium:** Video
- **Funnel:** All stages
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Trojan Horse, Reframe, This and a...
- **When to use:** When the visual needs to carry emotion while the voiceover carries logic.

### Text-on-Screen Video
Text overlays on video footage. No spoken words. Music or ambient sound.
- **Medium:** Video
- **Funnel:** All stages
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Contrast Without Comment, Implied Answer
- **When to use:** When viewers watch without sound. When text pacing creates tension.

### Stop Motion / Time-Lapse
Objects move or transform through compressed time.
- **Medium:** Video
- **Funnel:** Unaware, Problem-Aware
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Implied Answer, Contrast Without Comment
- **When to use:** When the process or transformation is visually satisfying.

### Unboxing
First-time product experience. Opening, discovering, reacting.
- **Medium:** Video
- **Funnel:** Product-Aware, Most-Aware
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Social Witness, Implied Answer
- **When to use:** Physical products where the packaging and reveal build desire.

## Static Formats

### Single Image
One image. Can be product shot, lifestyle, graphic, or photo.
- **Medium:** Static
- **Funnel:** All stages
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Implied Answer, Contrast Without Comment
- **When to use:** When one strong visual communicates the message. Simplest to produce.

### Quote Card
A testimonial, statistic, or statement presented as text on a designed background.
- **Medium:** Static
- **Funnel:** Product-Aware, Most-Aware
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Social Witness
- **When to use:** When the words are the creative. Best with real customer language.

### Infographic
Data or process visualized in a designed, scannable layout.
- **Medium:** Static
- **Funnel:** Problem-Aware, Solution-Aware
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Reframe, Contrast Without Comment
- **When to use:** When data is the differentiator and needs to be understood quickly.

### Carousel
Multiple images or slides swiped through sequentially.
- **Medium:** Static (multi-image)
- **Funnel:** All stages
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Trojan Horse (educational), Reframe (progressive reveal)
- **When to use:** When the story needs more than one frame but video is not justified.

### Comparison Grid
Side-by-side feature or benefit comparison.
- **Medium:** Static
- **Funnel:** Solution-Aware, Product-Aware
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Borrowed Enemy, Contrast Without Comment
- **When to use:** When buyers are actively comparing and need a clear matrix.

### Meme / Cultural Reference
Borrowed cultural format with brand-relevant twist.
- **Medium:** Static
- **Funnel:** Unaware, Problem-Aware
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Trojan Horse, Overheard Conversation
- **When to use:** When the audience is online-native and the reference is current.

### Text Post / Tweet Screenshot
Content formatted to look like a social post or conversation.
- **Medium:** Static
- **Funnel:** Unaware, Problem-Aware
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Overheard Conversation, Social Witness
- **When to use:** When the message lands better as "something someone said" than a brand statement.

### Annotated Screenshot
Product screenshot with callouts, arrows, or highlights.
- **Medium:** Static
- **Funnel:** Solution-Aware, Product-Aware
- **Best mechanic pairings:** Implied Answer, Contrast Without Comment
- **When to use:** Software products. When the feature is best shown in context with visual guidance.

## Format Selection by Goal

| If you want to... | Format |
|---|---|
| Build personal connection | Talking Head |
| Look authentic, not polished | UGC |
| Show the product working | Product Demo or Screen Recording |
| Show dramatic change | Before/After |
| Prove it with real reactions | Reaction / Response |
| Deep social proof | Interview / Testimonial |
| Make the pain relatable with humor | Skit / Scenario |
| Carry emotion + logic separately | Voiceover + B-Roll |
| Reach sound-off viewers | Text-on-Screen Video |
| Show a satisfying process | Stop Motion / Time-Lapse |
| Build desire through reveal | Unboxing |
| Communicate in one shot | Single Image |
| Let the words do the work | Quote Card |
| Make data digestible | Infographic |
| Tell a story in slides | Carousel |
| Help buyers compare | Comparison Grid |
| Tap into culture | Meme |
| Feel like organic content | Text Post / Tweet Screenshot |
| Show features in context | Annotated Screenshot |

## Platform Fit

| Platform | Preferred Formats |
|---|---|
| TikTok | UGC, Talking Head, Skit, Text-on-Screen, Reaction |
| Instagram Reels | UGC, Product Demo, Before/After, Voiceover + B-Roll |
| Instagram Feed | Carousel, Single Image, Quote Card, Meme |
| LinkedIn | Carousel, Single Image, Quote Card, Screen Recording |
| Facebook Feed | Single Image, Video (any), Carousel |
| YouTube Shorts | Talking Head, UGC, Before/After, Reaction |
| Google Display | Single Image, Annotated Screenshot |
