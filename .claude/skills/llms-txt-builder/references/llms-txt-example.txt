## Key Concepts

- **Workflow**: A reusable AI process that runs on schedule or trigger.
- **Connector**: An integration with an external service (Slack, GitHub, etc.).

## How It Works

1. Sign up with email — no credit card.
2. Pick a workflow template or start from scratch.
3. Connect your tools (Slack, GitHub, Linear, etc.).
4. Run the workflow on demand or schedule it.

Free tier covers 100 runs per month. Upgrade for unlimited.
