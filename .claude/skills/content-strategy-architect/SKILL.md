# Content Strategy Architect

## The "Help First" Principle
70% content that helps the audience do their job better. 30% content that promotes the product. Helpful content builds trust, earns links, ranks in search, and gets shared.

## Content by Funnel Stage

**Top (Awareness, 40%):** Educational blog posts, original research, templates/tools, video explainers.
**Middle (Consideration, 35%):** Comparison guides, case studies (A-side: polished visual success story; B-side: candid technical Q&A), how-to guides, webinars.
**Bottom (Decision, 25%):** Product demos, ROI calculators, implementation guides, pricing comparisons, testimonials.

## Distribution
Content without distribution is invisible. Spend 50% creating, 50% distributing.

**Owned:** Blog, email, docs, changelog.
**Social:** LinkedIn, Twitter/X, YouTube, Reddit, community forums.
**Earned:** Guest posts, podcasts, press, backlinks.
**Paid:** Sponsored content, social ads, search ads, retargeting.

## 12-Week Calendar

**Weeks 1-4 (Foundation):** 2x/week. Top-of-funnel SEO content. Build email list. Establish cadence.
**Weeks 5-8 (Depth):** Maintain 2x/week. Add one middle-of-funnel piece weekly. Start guest posting and community.
**Weeks 9-12 (Optimization):** Review what works. Double down on winners. Cut underperformers. Add bottom-of-funnel for highest-converting pages.

## Quality Checks
- [ ] Answers a real question the audience is asking
- [ ] Provides value even if the reader never buys
- [ ] Has a clear next action
- [ ] Better than current top 3 search results for the target query
