Day Trip to Krka from Vodice

Your Private Day Trip to Krka National Park

Staying in Vodice and looking for a taxi to Krka, or a simple round-trip transfer to the waterfalls and back? That is exactly what I offer. I am Antonio, a local driver and English speaker, not a tour agency. I pick you up right at the door, from any Vodice hotel, apartment or villa, the Vodice marina and Riva, and from Srima and Tribunj at the same price. I drive you to Krka National Park, give you three to four hours of free time to explore the waterfalls at your own pace, then bring you straight back. No shared groups, no guide talking over your day, no fixed schedule, just a private round-trip transfer and your day to enjoy.

In short: this is a private taxi transfer, not a group tour. You get the car, the local driver and the round trip from Vodice to Krka and back. What you do inside the park, and for how long, is entirely up to you.

How Your Round-Trip Transfer to Krka Works

1. Pickup at your door

On the morning of your trip I meet you at your chosen address in Vodice, Srima or Tribunj: a hotel, apartment, villa or the marina. No need to walk to a meeting point, I come to you.

2. Drive to Krka National Park

The drive from Vodice to Krka is short and scenic, around 30 minutes. On the way I explain the difference between the Skradin boat entrance and the Lozovac entrance and help you choose the best option for the season.

3. Free time inside the park

You get around 3 to 4 hours to explore the waterfalls, boardwalks and viewpoints at your own pace. This is a private transfer, not a guided group tour, so you are free to walk, take photos, have lunch or swim.

4. Return to Vodice

At the agreed time we meet back at the entrance and I drive you comfortably back to your hotel, apartment or villa in Vodice, Srima or Tribunj.

Krka Day Trip from Vodice: Frequently Asked Questions

Pickup & Logistics

Is this a guided tour or a taxi transfer?

It is a taxi transfer, not a guided tour. I am a local driver providing a private round-trip transfer from Vodice to Krka and back. You get the car and the driving; inside the park you are free to explore on your own, at your own pace.

Do you pick up from Srima and Tribunj as well as Vodice?

Yes. Pickup from Srima and Tribunj is at the same fixed price as Vodice. I collect you door to door from any hotel, apartment or villa, including the Vodice marina and Riva.

How long is the drive from Vodice to Krka?

Around 30 minutes to the Skradin or Lozovac entrance. It is a short, easy drive, so you get plenty of time at the waterfalls.

How long do we spend at Krka National Park?

Most guests choose around 3 to 4 hours of free time, enough to see the main waterfalls and walk the classic boardwalk loop. A shorter or longer stay can be agreed when you book.

Can children join this trip?

Yes, families are very welcome. I provide child and booster seats free of charge, just tell me the age of each child when booking.

Tickets & Booking

Are Krka entrance tickets included in the price?

No. Entrance tickets are bought separately, directly from Krka National Park at the Skradin or Lozovac entrance or online. This lets you choose the exact ticket type and any seasonal discount. Tickets cost roughly €7 to €40 depending on the time of year.

How do I book and pay for the day trip?

A 20% advance payment (minimum €20) confirms the booking, by bank transfer or card, and you get an invoice as confirmation. The balance is paid to me on the day, in cash or by card.

Can you drop us somewhere else after the trip?

In most cases, yes, a hotel change, the bus station or another town on the riviera. Just mention it in advance and I will confirm it fits the schedule.
