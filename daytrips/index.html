Krka National Park

Waterfalls Half an Hour from the Coast

Krka is the closest national park to Šibenik and the easiest half-day or full-day escape on this coast. The famous Skradinski Buk cascade, the Roški Slap falls, the monastery on Visovac island and kilometres of wooden boardwalks over clear green water are all inside. In summer you can swim near the falls, and the Skradin boat approach is a highlight in itself.

Because it is so close, a Krka day trip leaves plenty of the day free. I drop you at the Skradin or Lozovac entrance, you explore for three to four hours, and I have you back at your hotel, apartment or ship by early afternoon.

Plitvice Lakes

The Lakes Worth the Early Start

Plitvice Lakes National Park is the most famous natural wonder in Croatia: sixteen turquoise lakes tumbling into one another over travertine barriers, linked by wooden walkways and framed by dense forest. A panoramic electric boat and a road train carry you between the upper and lower lakes, both included with your park ticket.

It is further inland than Krka, so the day is longer, but going private means no dawn coach pickup and no rush. I collect you when it suits you, drive you straight there, and wait while you walk the lakes for as long as you like.

Day Trips from Šibenik: Frequently Asked Questions

Trips & Pickups

What day trips can I book?

The two most popular are Krka National Park and Plitvice Lakes. Both are private, fixed-price round-trip transfers with free time inside the park to explore at your own pace.

Are these guided tours or taxi transfers?

They are private taxi transfers, not guided group tours. I take you there and back in a comfortable Škoda Superb, and inside the park you explore on your own, at your own pace.

Do you pick up from other towns, not just Šibenik?

Yes. Krka runs from Šibenik, Vodice, Primošten, Zadar and Split, and Plitvice from Šibenik and Split. Each town has its own page and fixed price.

Prices & Booking

How much is a day trip?

A private Krka day trip starts at a fixed €100 per car from Šibenik. A Plitvice day trip is €500 per car from Šibenik. Prices are per car for up to 4 passengers and depend on your pickup town.

Are park entrance tickets included?

No. The price covers the private transfer and waiting time only. Krka and Plitvice tickets are bought separately at the entrance or online, roughly €7 to €40 depending on the season.

How do I book a day trip?

Message me on WhatsApp, call, or use the contact page. A 20% advance payment (minimum €20) confirms the date, and the balance is paid to me on the day.
