Day Trip to Krka from Split

Your Private Day Trip to Krka National Park

Looking for a taxi to Krka from Split, or a simple round-trip transfer to the waterfalls and back? That is exactly what I offer. I am Antonio, a local English-speaking driver, not a tour agency. I pick you up right at the door, from any Split hotel or apartment, the old town and Diocletian's Palace, the bus and train station, or directly at the cruise port and ferry terminal. I drive you to Krka National Park, give you three to four hours of free time to explore the waterfalls at your own pace, then bring you straight back to Split. No shared groups, no guide talking over your day, no fixed schedule, just a private round-trip transfer and your day to enjoy.

In short: this is a private taxi transfer from Split to Krka, not a group tour. You get the car, the local driver and the round trip to the waterfalls and back. What you do inside the park, and for how long, is entirely up to you.

How Your Round-Trip Transfer to Krka Works

1. Pickup in Split

On the morning of your trip I meet you at your chosen address in Split: a hotel, apartment, the old town, the bus or train station, or directly at the cruise port and ferry terminal. No need to walk to a meeting point, I come to you.

2. Drive to Krka National Park

The drive from Split to Krka is about an hour to an hour and fifteen along the coast and motorway. On the way I explain the difference between the Skradin boat entrance and the Lozovac entrance and help you choose the best one.

3. Free time inside the park

You get around 3 to 4 hours to explore the waterfalls, boardwalks and viewpoints at your own pace. This is a private transfer, not a guided group tour, so you are free to walk, take photos, have lunch or swim.

4. Return to Split

At the agreed time we meet back at the entrance and I drive you comfortably back to your hotel, apartment or ship in Split. For cruise passengers I plan extra buffer time so you are back well before departure.

Krka Day Trip from Split: Frequently Asked Questions

Pickup & Logistics

Is this a guided tour or a taxi transfer?

It is a taxi transfer, not a guided tour. I am a local driver providing a private round-trip transfer from Split to Krka and back. You get the car and the driving; inside the park you are free to explore on your own, at your own pace.

Do you pick up from Split hotels and the cruise port?

Yes. I pick up door to door from Split hotels and apartments, the old town, the bus and train station, and directly at the Split cruise port and ferry terminal, with a name sign on request.

How long does the day trip from Split to Krka take?

The drive is around one hour to one hour and fifteen minutes each way, plus about 3 to 4 hours of free time inside the park. A shorter or longer stay can be agreed when you book.

Can children join this trip?

Yes, families are very welcome. I provide child and booster seats free of charge, just tell me the age of each child when booking.

Tickets & Booking

How much is a taxi to Krka from Split?

A private round-trip transfer from Split to Krka is a fixed €290 per car for up to 4 passengers, with tolls, fuel and around 3 to 4 hours of waiting time included.

Are Krka entrance tickets included in the price?

No. Entrance tickets are bought separately, directly from Krka National Park at the Skradin or Lozovac entrance or online. Tickets cost roughly €7 to €40 depending on the time of year.

How do I book and pay for the day trip?

A 20% advance payment (minimum €20) confirms the booking, by bank transfer or card, and you get an invoice as confirmation. The balance is paid to me on the day, in cash or by card.
