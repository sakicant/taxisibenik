About Antonio Šakić

Your local taxi driver in Šibenik and Skradin.

Who Is Antonio?

I was born in Šibenik in 1993 and have lived in the area my entire life. I currently live in Piramatovci with my wife and two young children, right between Šibenik and Skradin.

I am not a call center, a booking platform, or a faceless company. I am a local person who knows these roads, these towns, and this coastline inside out.

Over a Decade Behind the Wheel

I have worked as a professional driver for more than ten years. My career began with local taxi services in Šibenik, expanded to Zagreb for one year, and included two winter seasons in Austria operating airport transfers from Salzburg, Innsbruck, Munich, and Klagenfurt to Alpine ski destinations.

I founded TAXI Antonio officially in May 2022. I believed there was space on the market for a driver who does things differently, someone who communicates clearly, shows up on time, and takes full responsibility.

Why I Work Differently

I operate without intermediaries between passengers and myself. I respond personally to messages, confirm bookings directly, and take responsibility for any issues. I built this website myself, and that same attention to detail extends to vehicle maintenance and customer service.

I want every passenger to feel like they have a local friend in Šibenik.

One Driver, One Car, One Standard

TAXI Antonio is a solo business with one Škoda Superb, accommodating up to four passengers with luggage. While I operate solo, I maintain a trusted network of reliable colleagues between Zadar and Split for emergencies, with transparent advance notification to passengers if this is ever needed.

Services I Offer

I cover airport transfers, city-to-city transfers, day trips to Krka National Park and Plitvice Lakes, and taxi services throughout the Dalmatian coast.
