Hotel & Marina

The Mandalina Peninsula, Door to Door

D-Resort Šibenik sits on the Mandalina peninsula, right beside the D-Marin Marina Mandalina, a few minutes from the old town. I pick you up at the hotel's main entrance, and if you are arriving or leaving by yacht, I collect you directly at the marina at the same fixed prices.

Booking in advance is what makes the price fixed. You tell me your date and time, I confirm the car and the meeting point, and the price you see is the price you pay. No taxi queue, no meter, no surge pricing in August.

D-Resort guests use me most for Split Airport runs, evenings in Šibenik old town and rides to the Krka waterfalls, but any destination in Croatia works. If it is not listed above, the quote form or a quick WhatsApp message gets you a fixed price in minutes.

D-Resort Villas

Doorstep Pickup at Your D-Resort Villa

Staying in one of the D-Resort villas rather than a hotel room? I drive right up to your villa. The villas are spread across the Mandalina peninsula, some a short walk from reception, so instead of hauling luggage to the main entrance I meet you at your own door at the time we agree.

It is the same fixed price and the same simple booking, whether you are in a villa, a suite or arriving by yacht at the marina next door. Just tell me your villa number when you book and I will find you.

FAQ

D-Resort Transfers: Frequently Asked Questions

Pickups & Airports

Where do you pick up at D-Resort?

Right at the hotel's main entrance on the Mandalina peninsula. If you are arriving by yacht at the D-Marin Marina Mandalina next door, I pick up at the marina too, at the same fixed prices.

Do you meet arriving guests at the airport?

Yes. I monitor your flight in real time, wait in the arrivals hall with a name sign, and drive you straight to D-Resort. Waiting for delayed flights is free.

Can you take us from D-Resort to Krka National Park?

Yes. A one-way transfer to the Lozovac entrance is €45 and to the Skradin entrance €50. I also offer a full private day trip with waiting time included.

Prices & Booking

How much is a taxi from D-Resort to Split Airport?

A fixed €95 per car for up to 4 passengers, booked in advance, the same in both directions. Tolls, fuel and luggage are included.

Should I book my transfer in advance?

Yes. The prices on this page are fixed prices for transfers booked in advance. Booking ahead guarantees the car, the pickup time and the price, which matters most for early flights and high season.

How do I pay?

A 20% advance payment (minimum €20) confirms the booking, by bank transfer or card. The balance is paid to me on the day, in cash or by card.
