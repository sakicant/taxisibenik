The Whole Resort Covered

Every Amadria Park Hotel and the Camp

The Amadria Park resort sits on the coast just south of Šibenik, and I drive to and from it almost every day of the season. Whichever hotel you are staying in, I pick you up at its front entrance, and for campers I meet you at the Amadria Park Camp reception. The fixed prices on this page apply to the whole resort.

Booking in advance is what makes the price fixed. You tell me your hotel, date and time, I confirm the car and the exact meeting point, and the price you see is the price you pay. No taxi queue at reception, no meter, no surge pricing in August.

Resort guests use me most for Split Airport runs, evenings in Šibenik old town and rides to the Krka waterfalls, but any destination in Croatia works. If it is not listed above, the quote form or a quick WhatsApp message gets you a fixed price in minutes.

FAQ

Amadria Park Transfers: Frequently Asked Questions

Pickups & Airports

Do you pick up from all Amadria Park hotels and the camp?

Yes. I pick up at the door of every hotel in the resort and at the Amadria Park Camp reception, at the same fixed prices. Tell me your hotel name when booking and I will confirm the exact meeting point.

Do you meet arriving guests at the airport?

Yes. I monitor your flight in real time, wait in the arrivals hall with a name sign, and drive you straight to your hotel or the camp. Waiting for delayed flights is free.

Can you take us from Amadria Park to Krka National Park?

Yes. A one-way transfer to the Lozovac entrance is €45 and to the Skradin entrance €50. I also offer a full private day trip with waiting time included.

Prices & Booking

How much is a taxi from Amadria Park to Split Airport?

A fixed €95 per car for up to 4 passengers, booked in advance, the same in both directions. Tolls, fuel and luggage are included.

Should I book my transfer in advance?

Yes. The prices on this page are fixed prices for transfers booked in advance. Booking ahead guarantees the car, the pickup time and the price, which matters most for early flights and high season.

How do I pay?

A 20% advance payment (minimum €20) confirms the booking, by bank transfer or card. The balance is paid to me on the day, in cash or by card.
