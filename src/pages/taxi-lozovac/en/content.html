Your Taxi to Krka National Park at Lozovac

Lozovac is the main gateway to Krka National Park, the road entrance that leads down to the Skradinski Buk waterfalls, so most trips here are about the park. I run day-trip and excursion transfers to and from the Lozovac and Skradin entrances, airport pickups for visitors who base themselves near the falls, and runs into Šibenik and up to Vodice. If you want the full round trip with waiting time while you explore, I offer that too, at a fixed price with doorstep pickup.

How My Lozovac Taxi Works

Šibenik-Based, Booked Ahead for Lozovac

To be clear, I am a private taxi based in the Šibenik and Skradin area, not a rank cab parked in Lozovac. I come to you for pre-arranged transfers, so booking ahead is how it works. I am not the driver to flag down for a two-minute hop around Lozovac.

What I do run from Lozovac are the trips worth booking a car for: the ride into Šibenik or Skradin, airport transfers to Split and Zadar, and longer journeys across Croatia. These private transfers typically start from around €25 and go up with the distance.

I also take Lozovac guests to Krka National Park, either the Lozovac road entrance or the Skradin boat entrance, one way or as a full round-trip day out while I wait. Airport transfers are the rides I am asked for most, so tell me your flight and I will confirm a fixed price.

FAQ

Taxi Lozovac: Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Lozovac from the Krka entrance?

Lozovac is the Krka National Park road entrance itself, so you are already at the gate for the Skradinski Buk waterfalls. I drop you right at the entrance.

Can you wait while I visit the park?

Yes. I offer a private Krka day trip with waiting time included, so I bring you to the falls, wait while you explore at your own pace, and drive you back. See my day trips page.

Do you also go to the Skradin entrance?

Yes. A transfer to the Krka Skradin entrance, where you take the boat to the falls, is a fixed €25 per car.
