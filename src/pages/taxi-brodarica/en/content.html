Your Local Taxi in Brodarica

Brodarica is the coastal village just south of Šibenik, best known as the crossing point for Krapanj, the smallest inhabited island in the Adriatic and only a short ferry hop across the channel. I meet guests right on the Brodarica waterfront by the ferry landing, handle the airport runs to Split and Zadar, and cover the quick trip into Šibenik. Door to door in a Škoda Superb, at a fixed price you know before you travel.

How My Brodarica Taxi Works

Šibenik-Based, Booked Ahead for Brodarica

To be clear, I am a private taxi based in the Šibenik and Skradin area, not a rank cab parked in Brodarica. I come to you for pre-arranged transfers, so booking ahead is how it works. I am not the driver to flag down for a two-minute hop around Brodarica.

What I do run from Brodarica are the trips worth booking a car for: the ride into Šibenik or Skradin, airport transfers to Split and Zadar, and longer journeys across Croatia. These private transfers typically start from around €25 and go up with the distance.

I also take Brodarica guests to Krka National Park, either the Lozovac road entrance or the Skradin boat entrance, one way or as a full round-trip day out while I wait. Airport transfers are the rides I am asked for most, so tell me your flight and I will confirm a fixed price.

Taxi Brodarica: Frequently Asked Questions

Can you drop me at the Krapanj ferry?

Yes. I take you right down to the Brodarica waterfront by the ferry landing for the Krapanj crossing, and I can collect you there on the way back too.

Do you serve the whole Brodarica waterfront?

Yes, along the seafront and the streets behind it. Send your address or accommodation name and I will confirm the exact meeting point.

Is the airport transfer a fixed price?

Yes. Split Airport is €95 and Zadar Airport €135 per car for up to 4 passengers, tolls and luggage included.
