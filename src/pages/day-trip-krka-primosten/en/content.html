How Your Round-Trip Transfer to Krka Works

1. Pickup at your door

On the morning of your trip I meet you at your chosen address in Primošten, the Zečevo area or Rogoznica: a hotel, apartment, villa or the marina. No need to walk to a meeting point, I come to you.

2. Drive to Krka National Park

The drive from Primošten to Krka is a scenic coastal route of around 45 minutes. On the way I explain the difference between the Skradin boat entrance and the Lozovac entrance and help you choose the best option for the season.

3. Free time inside the park

You get around 3 to 4 hours to explore the waterfalls, boardwalks and viewpoints at your own pace. This is a private transfer, not a guided group tour, so you are free to walk, take photos, have lunch or swim.

4. Return to Primošten

At the agreed time we meet back at the entrance and I drive you comfortably back to your hotel, apartment or villa in Primošten, the Zečevo area or Rogoznica.

Krka Day Trip from Primošten: Frequently Asked Questions

Pickup & Logistics

Is this a guided tour or a taxi transfer?

It is a taxi transfer, not a guided tour. I am a local driver providing a private round-trip transfer from Primošten to Krka and back. You get the car and the driving; inside the park you are free to explore on your own, at your own pace.

Do you pick up from the Zečevo area and Rogoznica as well as Primošten?

Yes. Pickup from the Zečevo area and Rogoznica is at the same fixed price as Primošten. I collect you door to door from any hotel, apartment or villa, including Marina Frapa in Rogoznica.

How long is the drive from Primošten to Krka?

Around 45 minutes along the coast to the Skradin or Lozovac entrance. It is an easy, scenic drive with plenty of time left for the waterfalls.

How long do we spend at Krka National Park?

Most guests choose around 3 to 4 hours of free time, enough to see the main waterfalls and walk the classic boardwalk loop. A shorter or longer stay can be agreed when you book.

Can children join this trip?

Yes, families are very welcome. I provide child and booster seats free of charge, just tell me the age of each child when booking.

Tickets & Booking

Are Krka entrance tickets included in the price?

No. Entrance tickets are bought separately, directly from Krka National Park at the Skradin or Lozovac entrance or online. This lets you choose the exact ticket type and any seasonal discount. Tickets cost roughly €7 to €40 depending on the time of year.

How do I book and pay for the day trip?

A 20% advance payment (minimum €20) confirms the booking, by bank transfer or card, and you get an invoice as confirmation. The balance is paid to me on the day, in cash or by card.

Can you drop us somewhere else after the trip?

In most cases, yes, a hotel change, the bus station or another town along the coast. Just mention it in advance and I will confirm it fits the schedule.
