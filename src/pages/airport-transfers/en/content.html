Private vs. Shuttle

Why Book a Private Airport Transfer Instead of a Shuttle or Bus?

Croatia's public transport between airports and the coast is inconvenient at best. Buses from Split Airport run infrequently, stop in multiple towns, and never go door to door, so you still need another ride at the end. Shared shuttles sound convenient until your fellow passengers have different drop-offs and a 90-minute transfer turns into a three-hour ordeal with luggage.

A private airport transfer from Šibenik means I pick you up at your exact address and drive you directly to the terminal, or meet you in arrivals and take you straight to your accommodation. My fixed price covers up to 4 passengers in a Škoda Superb, so the more of you there are, the better the value per person compared to any shuttle or public option.

I monitor every incoming flight in real time. If your plane is delayed, I adjust automatically and wait at no extra charge. That guarantee alone is worth more than the price difference.

FAQ

Šibenik Airport Transfers: Frequently Asked Questions

Booking & Payment

How do I find my driver at the airport?

I wait in the arrivals hall after baggage claim, holding a clear sign with your name.

Is the airport transfer price fixed?

Yes, a fixed price per car for up to 4 passengers, with tolls, fuel and luggage included. No meter, no surprises.

How do I pay?

A 20% advance payment (minimum €20) confirms the booking, by bank transfer or card. The balance is paid to me on the day, in cash or by card.

Flights & Extras

What if my flight is delayed?

I monitor your flight in real time using the flight number you give at booking, adjust your pickup automatically, and wait at no extra charge.

Is there free cancellation?

Cancellations more than 72 hours before are fully refundable (minus a €10 card fee if you paid by card). Within 72 hours or no-shows are non-refundable, but you can reschedule any time at no charge.

Do you provide child seats?

Yes, child and booster seats are free on request. Just mention the child's age in your booking notes.
