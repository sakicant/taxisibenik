Your Local Taxi in Vodice

Vodice is one of the busiest resort towns on this coast, with a large marina, a lively summer nightlife strip and ferry links out to the islands of Prvić and Zlarin. It is one of my biggest markets and I cover all of it: yacht and marina crews arriving for a charter, hotel and apartment guests, groups heading out for the evening, and the airport transfers that bookend every holiday. Split and Zadar airports are both an easy fixed-price run, and Krka's waterfalls are close by. Doorstep pickup, a comfortable Škoda Superb, and the price agreed up front.

Taxi Vodice: Frequently Asked Questions

Can you pick up crews at Vodice marina?

Yes. I meet yacht and charter crews right at the marina with space for luggage and provisions, and I can arrange an early or late slot around your charter.

Do you run late at night in Vodice?

Yes. If it is booked ahead I can collect you after an evening out and get you back to your accommodation safely at a fixed price.

Can you take me to the Prvić or Zlarin ferry?

Yes. Both leave from the Vodice waterfront and I can drop you right at the landing. Ask me for the fare when you book.

How much is Vodice to Zadar Airport?

A fixed €100 per car for up to 4 passengers, and Split Airport is €115, tolls and luggage included.
