How Your Round-Trip Transfer to Krka Works

1. Pickup in Zadar

On the morning of your trip I meet you at your chosen address in Zadar: a hotel, apartment, the old town, the bus station, or directly at the ferry port and the Gaženica cruise terminal. No need to walk to a meeting point, I come to you.

2. Drive to Krka National Park

The drive from Zadar to Krka is about an hour on the motorway. On the way I explain the difference between the Skradin boat entrance and the Lozovac entrance and help you choose the best one for the day.

3. Free time inside the park

You get around 3 to 4 hours to explore the waterfalls, boardwalks and viewpoints at your own pace. This is a private transfer, not a guided group tour, so you are free to walk, take photos, have lunch or swim.

4. Return to Zadar

At the agreed time we meet back at the entrance and I drive you comfortably back to your hotel, apartment or ship in Zadar. For cruise passengers I plan extra buffer time so you are back well before departure.
