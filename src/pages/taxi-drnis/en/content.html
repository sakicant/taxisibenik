Your Intercity Taxi in Drniš

Drniš is an inland town in the Krka river valley, famous for its air-dried prosciutto and as a gateway to the upper reaches of the park. Trips from Drniš are longer, intercity runs rather than short beach hops: down to Šibenik and the coast, or straight to Split airport for a flight. I drive the whole way in a comfortable Škoda Superb at a fixed price, with room for luggage and a stop for a coffee or a photo along the way if you like.

How My Drniš Taxi Works

Šibenik-Based, Booked Ahead for Drniš

To be clear, I am a private taxi based in the Šibenik and Skradin area, not a rank cab parked in Drniš. I come to you for pre-arranged transfers, so booking ahead is how it works. I am not the driver to flag down for a two-minute hop around Drniš.

What I do run from Drniš are the trips worth booking a car for: the ride into Šibenik or Skradin, airport transfers to Split and Zadar, and longer journeys across Croatia. These private transfers typically start from around €65 and go up with the distance.

I also take Drniš guests to Krka National Park, either the Lozovac road entrance or the Skradin boat entrance, one way or as a full round-trip day out while I wait. Airport transfers are the rides I am asked for most, so tell me your flight and I will confirm a fixed price.
