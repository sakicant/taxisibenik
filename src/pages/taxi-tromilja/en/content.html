Your Local Taxi in Tromilja

Tromilja is a small inland settlement near Bilice, a short way off the Šibenik to Drniš road. Trips here are mostly practical: a run into Šibenik, or an airport transfer to Split or Zadar at the start or end of a visit. It is also a quiet, handy base for a day at the Krka waterfalls. I pick you up at the door at a fixed, agreed price, with a comfortable Škoda Superb and room for luggage.
