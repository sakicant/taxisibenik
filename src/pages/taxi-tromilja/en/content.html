Your Local Taxi in Tromilja

Tromilja is a small inland settlement near Bilice, a short way off the Šibenik to Drniš road. Trips here are mostly practical: a run into Šibenik, or an airport transfer to Split or Zadar at the start or end of a visit. It is also a quiet, handy base for a day at the Krka waterfalls. I pick you up at the door at a fixed, agreed price, with a comfortable Škoda Superb and room for luggage.

How My Tromilja Taxi Works

Šibenik-Based, Booked Ahead for Tromilja

To be clear, I am a private taxi based in the Šibenik and Skradin area, not a rank cab parked in Tromilja. I come to you for pre-arranged transfers, so booking ahead is how it works. I am not the driver to flag down for a two-minute hop around Tromilja.

What I do run from Tromilja are the trips worth booking a car for: the ride into Šibenik or Skradin, airport transfers to Split and Zadar, and longer journeys across Croatia. These private transfers typically start from around €40 and go up with the distance.

I also take Tromilja guests to Krka National Park, either the Lozovac road entrance or the Skradin boat entrance, one way or as a full round-trip day out while I wait. Airport transfers are the rides I am asked for most, so tell me your flight and I will confirm a fixed price.
