How Your Day Trip to Krka Works

1. Pickup at your door

On the morning of your trip I meet you at your chosen address in Šibenik: a hotel, apartment, the bus or train station, or directly at the ferry and cruise port. No need to walk to a meeting point, I come to you.

2. Drive to Krka National Park

The drive from Šibenik to Krka is short and scenic. On the way I explain the difference between the Skradin boat entrance and the Lozovac entrance and help you choose the best option for the season.

3. Free time inside the park

You get around 3 to 4 hours to explore the waterfalls, boardwalks and viewpoints at your own pace. This is a private transfer, not a guided group tour, so you are free to walk, take photos, have lunch or swim.

4. Return to Šibenik

At the agreed time we meet back at the entrance and I drive you comfortably back to your hotel, apartment or ship. For cruise passengers I plan extra buffer time so you are back well before departure.

Krka Day Trip from Šibenik: Frequently Asked Questions

Pickup & Logistics

Can you pick us up from Amadria Park, D-Resort or Hotel Bellevue?

Yes. I regularly pick up guests from Amadria Park resort, D-Resort Šibenik, Hotel Bellevue and other Šibenik hotels. Send me your hotel name and I will confirm the exact pickup time and meeting point.

Do you pick up from the ferry port, bus or train station?

Yes. I collect guests door to door from the Šibenik ferry and cruise port, the bus station and the Šibenik and Perković train stations, with a name sign on request.

How long do we spend at Krka National Park?

Most guests choose around 3 to 4 hours of free time, enough to see the main waterfalls and walk the classic boardwalk loop. A shorter or longer stay can be agreed when you book.

Can children join this trip?

Yes, families are very welcome. I provide child and booster seats free of charge, just tell me the age of each child when booking.

Tickets & Booking

Are Krka entrance tickets included in the price?

No. Entrance tickets are bought separately, directly from Krka National Park at the Skradin or Lozovac entrance or online. This lets you choose the exact ticket type and any seasonal discount. Tickets cost roughly €7 to €40 depending on the time of year.

How do I book and pay for the day trip?

A 20% advance payment (minimum €20) confirms the booking, by bank transfer or card, and you get an invoice as confirmation. The balance is paid to me on the day, in cash or by card.

Can you drop us somewhere else after the trip?

In most cases, yes, a hotel change, the bus station or the port. Just mention it in advance and I will confirm it fits the schedule.
