Your Local Taxi in Jadrija

Jadrija is Šibenik's own little beach at the tip of the peninsula, with its row of old bathing cabins and the lighthouse at the channel mouth. Trips here are mostly short and local rather than long distance: a lift back into town, a run to the bus and train station, or a transfer out to Split or Zadar airport when your stay ends. I come to you and keep the price fixed and agreed up front.

How My Jadrija Taxi Works

Šibenik-Based, Booked Ahead for Jadrija

To be clear, I am a private taxi based in the Šibenik and Skradin area, not a rank cab parked in Jadrija. I come to you for pre-arranged transfers, so booking ahead is how it works. I am not the driver to flag down for a two-minute hop around Jadrija.

What I do run from Jadrija are the trips worth booking a car for: the ride into Šibenik or Skradin, airport transfers to Split and Zadar, and longer journeys across Croatia. These private transfers typically start from around €30 and go up with the distance.

I also take Jadrija guests to Krka National Park, either the Lozovac road entrance or the Skradin boat entrance, one way or as a full round-trip day out while I wait. Airport transfers are the rides I am asked for most, so tell me your flight and I will confirm a fixed price.

FAQ

Taxi Jadrija: Frequently Asked Questions

Is Jadrija mostly short local rides?

Yes, most trips are short hops into Šibenik or to the station, but I also run the longer airport transfers to Split and Zadar when you need them.

Can you take me to the bus or train station?

Yes. The Šibenik bus and train stations sit next to each other, and a transfer from Jadrija is a fixed €30 per car.
