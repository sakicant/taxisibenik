Your Local Taxi in Jadrija

Jadrija is Šibenik's own little beach at the tip of the peninsula, with its row of old bathing cabins and the lighthouse at the channel mouth. Trips here are mostly short and local rather than long distance: a lift back into town, a run to the bus and train station, or a transfer out to Split or Zadar airport when your stay ends. I come to you and keep the price fixed and agreed up front.

FAQ

Taxi Jadrija: Frequently Asked Questions

Is Jadrija mostly short local rides?

Yes, most trips are short hops into Šibenik or to the station, but I also run the longer airport transfers to Split and Zadar when you need them.

Can you take me to the bus or train station?

Yes. The Šibenik bus and train stations sit next to each other, and a transfer from Jadrija is a fixed €30 per car.
