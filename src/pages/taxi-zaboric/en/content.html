Your Local Taxi in Žaborić

Žaborić is a small, quiet beach village on the coast road between Brodarica and Grebaštica, mostly private apartments and a gentle pebble shore. It is a calm place to stay, but that also means no local taxi rank, so a booked transfer is the sensible way to reach Split or Zadar airport, or to pop into Šibenik and Vodice. I collect you at your accommodation and keep the fare fixed and agreed before we set off.

How My Žaborić Taxi Works

Šibenik-Based, Booked Ahead for Žaborić

To be clear, I am a private taxi based in the Šibenik and Skradin area, not a rank cab parked in Žaborić. I come to you for pre-arranged transfers, so booking ahead is how it works. I am not the driver to flag down for a two-minute hop around Žaborić.

What I do run from Žaborić are the trips worth booking a car for: the ride into Šibenik or Skradin, airport transfers to Split and Zadar, and longer journeys across Croatia. These private transfers typically start from around €25 and go up with the distance.

I also take Žaborić guests to Krka National Park, either the Lozovac road entrance or the Skradin boat entrance, one way or as a full round-trip day out while I wait. Airport transfers are the rides I am asked for most, so tell me your flight and I will confirm a fixed price.
