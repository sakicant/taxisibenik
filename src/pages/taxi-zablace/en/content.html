Your Local Taxi in Zablaće

Zablaće is the low, sandy peninsula southwest of Šibenik, a favourite with campers and beach tourists and close to the Amadria Park resort. Whether you are under canvas, in an apartment, or staying at Amadria Park nearby, I handle the airport transfers to Split and Zadar and the shorter runs into Šibenik and up the coast to Vodice. Doorstep pickup, room for the camping gear, one fixed price per car agreed before we drive.

How My Zablaće Taxi Works

Šibenik-Based, Booked Ahead for Zablaće

To be clear, I am a private taxi based in the Šibenik and Skradin area, not a rank cab parked in Zablaće. I come to you for pre-arranged transfers, so booking ahead is how it works. I am not the driver to flag down for a two-minute hop around Zablaće.

What I do run from Zablaće are the trips worth booking a car for: the ride into Šibenik or Skradin, airport transfers to Split and Zadar, and longer journeys across Croatia. These private transfers typically start from around €18 and go up with the distance.

I also take Zablaće guests to Krka National Park, either the Lozovac road entrance or the Skradin boat entrance, one way or as a full round-trip day out while I wait. Airport transfers are the rides I am asked for most, so tell me your flight and I will confirm a fixed price.
