Taxi Lozovac to Zagreb Airport: Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Zagreb Airport from Lozovac, and how long is the drive?

The drive from Lozovac to Zagreb Airport is around 352 km and takes roughly 3 h 50 min in normal traffic, a little longer at busy times in summer. I plan the pickup so a slow patch never leaves you rushed.

How much is a taxi from Lozovac to Zagreb Airport?

A fixed €485 per car, one way, for up to 4 passengers. The price is per vehicle, not per person, and includes tolls, fuel and luggage. A return trip is €970.

What time should I leave Lozovac for my flight?

As a rule, about 3 hours before an international flight and 2 hours before a domestic one. Tell me your flight time and I will confirm the exact pickup.

Do you drop us right at the departures terminal?

Yes. I take you straight to the departures drop-off at Zagreb Airport and help with your luggage. No parking walk, no shuttle.

How far in advance should I book?

At least 2 hours ahead so I can confirm the car and your pickup, and earlier for early-morning flights and high season. For a sooner ride, call or WhatsApp me directly.
