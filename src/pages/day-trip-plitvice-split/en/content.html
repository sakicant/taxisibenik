How Your Round-Trip Transfer to Plitvice Works

1. Pickup in Split

Early on the morning of your trip I meet you at your chosen address in Split: a hotel, apartment, the old town, the bus or train station, or directly at the cruise port and ferry terminal. No need to walk to a meeting point, I come to you.

2. Drive to Plitvice Lakes

The drive from Split to Plitvice is around two and a half to three hours each way on the motorway. On the way I share tips on the entrances and the best walking route for the season.

3. Free time inside the park

You get several hours to walk the wooden boardwalks, ride the electric boat and panoramic train, and take in the waterfalls at your own pace. This is a private transfer, not a guided group tour.

4. Return to Split

At the agreed time we meet back at the entrance and I drive you comfortably back to your hotel, apartment or ship in Split. Plitvice from Split is a long day, so we usually return in the evening.

The Lakes

What You'll See at Plitvice Lakes

Plitvice is a chain of sixteen terraced lakes in every shade of turquoise and green, linked by tumbling waterfalls and kilometres of wooden boardwalks. Your park ticket includes the electric boat across the largest lake and the panoramic shuttle train, so you can cover the upper and lower lakes without backtracking.

Because I drop you at the entrance and wait, you set the pace: the short lower-lakes loop to the Veliki Slap waterfall, or a longer day taking in the quieter upper lakes. I will point you to the best route for the day and the season before you set off.
