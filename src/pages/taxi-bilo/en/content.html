Your Local Taxi in Bilo

Bilo is a small beach settlement on the coast between Grebaštica and Primošten, a quiet place of apartments and pebble coves. Like its neighbours it has no local taxi rank, so the reliable way to reach Split airport, or to get into Šibenik and up to Vodice, is a booked transfer. I meet you at your accommodation and keep the fare fixed and agreed before we drive, in a comfortable Škoda Superb with room for your luggage.

How My Bilo Taxi Works

Šibenik-Based, Booked Ahead for Bilo

To be clear, I am a private taxi based in the Šibenik and Skradin area, not a rank cab parked in Bilo. I come to you for pre-arranged transfers, so booking ahead is how it works. I am not the driver to flag down for a two-minute hop around Bilo.

What I do run from Bilo are the trips worth booking a car for: the ride into Šibenik or Skradin, airport transfers to Split and Zadar, and longer journeys across Croatia. These private transfers typically start from around €40 and go up with the distance.

I also take Bilo guests to Krka National Park, either the Lozovac road entrance or the Skradin boat entrance, one way or as a full round-trip day out while I wait. Airport transfers are the rides I am asked for most, so tell me your flight and I will confirm a fixed price.
