Your Local Taxi in Bilo

Bilo is a small beach settlement on the coast between Grebaštica and Primošten, a quiet place of apartments and pebble coves. Like its neighbours it has no local taxi rank, so the reliable way to reach Split airport, or to get into Šibenik and up to Vodice, is a booked transfer. I meet you at your accommodation and keep the fare fixed and agreed before we drive, in a comfortable Škoda Superb with room for your luggage.
