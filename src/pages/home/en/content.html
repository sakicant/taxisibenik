Booking and Pricing

How do I book a taxi from Šibenik?

Call +385 99 447 1013, message me on WhatsApp, or send an email. I need your pickup location, destination, and preferred pickup or arrival time.

How far in advance should I book?

I take last-minute requests within my working hours, but booking in advance guarantees availability.

What does "fixed price" mean? What's included?

My fixed prices include transportation, fuel, tolls, and help with luggage. No hidden fees. Extra stops or long waits may cost more, and I'll always tell you upfront.

Do you take advance payments?

Yes. Confirming a booking requires a 20% advance payment (minimum €20). Rides priced at €20 or below require full payment in advance. You can also pay the full amount upfront if you prefer. Advance payments can be made by bank transfer (SEPA) or card, with the remaining balance settled with me on the day of the transfer.

Do you accept card payments in the car?

Yes, I accept cards and other contactless payment methods.

Flight Delays and Cancellations

What if my flight is delayed?

I monitor flight arrivals in real time and adjust your pickup time automatically, at no extra cost.

What happens if I need to cancel?

Cancellations must be made in writing by email. More than 72 hours before your booking, you get a full refund if you paid by bank transfer, or a full refund minus a €10 processing fee if you paid by card. Cancellations within 72 hours, or no-shows, are non-refundable. You can reschedule at any time at no extra charge, your payment carries over in full to the new date.

Can you accommodate a larger group?

Yes, my Škoda Superb fits up to 4 passengers. For larger groups, I can arrange it through local, hand-picked partners I regularly work with. Just contact me for details.

Do you offer child seats?

Yes, I provide child and booster seats on request, just mention it when booking.
