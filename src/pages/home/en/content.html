About TAXI Antonio

Your Taxi Šibenik Service, Run Personally by Antonio

TAXI Antonio is a locally-operated taxi and transfer service based in Šibenik, run by Antonio Šakić with over a decade of experience on the roads of Dalmatia. When you call, you speak directly with Antonio, not a call center, not a middleman, just clear and direct communication from booking to drop-off.

Riding in a comfortable, air-conditioned Škoda Superb with complimentary Wi-Fi, you can count on punctual airport pickups with real-time flight monitoring, safe transfers across the region, and knowledgeable day trips to Croatia's most beautiful national parks.
