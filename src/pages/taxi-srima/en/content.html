How My Srima Taxi Works

Šibenik-Based, Booked Ahead for Srima

To be clear, I am a private taxi based in the Šibenik and Skradin area, not a rank cab parked in Srima. I come to you for pre-arranged transfers, so booking ahead is how it works. I am not the driver to flag down for a two-minute hop around Srima.

What I do run from Srima are the trips worth booking a car for: the ride into Šibenik or Skradin, airport transfers to Split and Zadar, and longer journeys across Croatia. These private transfers typically start from around €25 and go up with the distance.

I also take Srima guests to Krka National Park, either the Lozovac road entrance or the Skradin boat entrance, one way or as a full round-trip day out while I wait. Airport transfers are the rides I am asked for most, so tell me your flight and I will confirm a fixed price.
