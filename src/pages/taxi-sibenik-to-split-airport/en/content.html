The Drive to the Airport

Šibenik to Split Airport, Step by Step

Split Airport (SPU) sits at Resnik near Kaštela, around 52 kilometres south of Šibenik. The drive takes roughly 50 to 60 minutes on the A1 motorway and the coastal approach, a little longer at peak times in July and August when the Trogir stretch gets busy. I factor that seasonal traffic into your pickup time so a slow patch never costs you your flight.

For a smooth departure I usually suggest leaving Šibenik about 3 hours before an international flight, or 2 hours before a domestic one. That covers the drive, drop-off, check-in and security with a comfortable buffer. If you are travelling light with only hand luggage, we can trim that a little. Send me your flight number and time and I will confirm the exact pickup.

On the day I arrive at your door a few minutes early, load your bags, and take you straight to the departures terminal. No parking, no shuttle bus, no walk with the suitcases.
