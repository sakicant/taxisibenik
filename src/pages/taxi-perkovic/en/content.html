Your Taxi at Perković Railway Station

Perković is the main railway junction for the Šibenik area, the station where the Šibenik branch line meets the Zagreb to Split route, so many travellers arrive here by train and need a transfer onward. I meet you on the platform, help with the bags, and drive you straight to where you are going: Šibenik, Vodice, the Krka waterfalls, Drniš, Knin or Split airport. I track your train so I am waiting when it pulls in, even if it runs late. Tell me your route and I will confirm a fixed price before you travel.

How My Perković Taxi Works

Šibenik-Based, Booked Ahead for Perković

To be clear, I am a private taxi based in the Šibenik and Skradin area, not a rank cab parked in Perković. I come to you for pre-arranged transfers, so booking ahead is how it works. I am not the driver to flag down for a two-minute hop around Perković.

What I do run from Perković are the trips worth booking a car for: the ride into Šibenik or Skradin, airport transfers to Split and Zadar, and longer journeys across Croatia, at a fixed price agreed in advance.

I also take Perković guests to Krka National Park, either the Lozovac road entrance or the Skradin boat entrance, one way or as a full round-trip day out while I wait. Airport transfers are the rides I am asked for most, so tell me your flight and I will confirm a fixed price.

FAQ

Taxi Perković: Frequently Asked Questions

Where do you meet me at Perković station?

On the platform, or by the station exit if you prefer. I will have your name ready and help with your bags so you can walk straight to the car.

What if my train to Perković is late?

I track your train and adjust the pickup to match, at no extra charge, so I am there whenever it actually arrives.

Is there a taxi rank at Perković?

No, there is no rank at the station, so it is best to book ahead. Send me your train time and destination and I will be waiting when you step off.
