In the Heart of Šibenik

Pickup in Front of the Hotel

The Bellevue Superior Hotel sits in the centre of Šibenik, steps from the old town, and I know its entrance and the surrounding streets like my own driveway. I confirm the exact pickup spot when you book, pull up in front of the hotel at the agreed time, and load your luggage while you finish your coffee.

Booking in advance is what makes the price fixed. You tell me your date and time, I confirm the car and the meeting point, and the price you see is the price you pay. No taxi queue, no meter, no surge pricing in August.

Bellevue guests use me most for Split Airport runs, trips to the Krka waterfalls and evenings in Vodice or Primošten, but any destination in Croatia works. If it is not listed above, the quote form or a quick WhatsApp message gets you a fixed price in minutes.

FAQ

Bellevue Transfers: Frequently Asked Questions

Pickups & Airports

Where do you pick up at the Bellevue Superior Hotel?

Right in front of the hotel. The Bellevue is in the heart of Šibenik, and I confirm the exact pickup spot when you book so you are not waiting at the kerb.

Do you meet arriving guests at the airport?

Yes. I monitor your flight in real time, wait in the arrivals hall with a name sign, and drive you straight to the Bellevue. Waiting for delayed flights is free.

Can you take us from the Bellevue to Krka National Park?

Yes. A one-way transfer to the Lozovac entrance is €45 and to the Skradin entrance €50. I also offer a full private day trip with waiting time included.

Prices & Booking

How much is a taxi from the Bellevue to Split Airport?

A fixed €95 per car for up to 4 passengers, booked in advance, the same in both directions. Tolls, fuel and luggage are included.

Should I book my transfer in advance?

Yes. The prices on this page are fixed prices for transfers booked in advance. Booking ahead guarantees the car, the pickup time and the price, which matters most for early flights and high season.

How do I pay?

A 20% advance payment (minimum €20) confirms the booking, by bank transfer or card. The balance is paid to me on the day, in cash or by card.
